Executive Summary

The Enterprise Portal is approximately 35% complete against an August 31 general availability date. Project status is AMBER: backend services are ahead of schedule, but the frontend design handoff from Product Design (T002) has been delayed two weeks due to Design System v2 prioritisation. A revised handoff date of 2025-05-15 has been agreed with Casey Liu (E006). No impact to GA date is anticipated if the revised date is met.

Scope

The Enterprise Portal delivers a self-service B2B administration interface serving our 120 enterprise accounts. Capabilities include: user provisioning and de-provisioning, role and permission management, usage reporting and cost allocation, billing overview, and API key management. It is a key Q3 sales enablement tool, expected to reduce enterprise onboarding time from 14 days to 3 days.
