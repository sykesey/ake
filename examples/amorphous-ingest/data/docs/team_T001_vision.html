Core Platform Team Vision 2025

Team ID: T001 | Department: Engineering | Lead: Jordan Kim (E001) | Headcount: 5

Mission

The Core Platform team builds the foundational infrastructure that enables every product team to ship faster, safer, and with greater confidence. Our systems underpin authentication, data access, and the developer experience across all of engineering.

Strategic Goals for 2025

  • Complete the API v3 Migration (PR001) by end of Q2, retiring 47 legacy endpoints
  • Launch the Enterprise Portal (PR006) to serve the B2B customer segment by Q3
  • Reduce internal API latency p99 from 480 ms to under 120 ms
  • Achieve 99.95% uptime across all core services
  • Ship an internal developer portal with self-service credential management

Team Principles

We build platforms, not products. Every decision we make should multiply the output of the teams that depend on us. We invest in observability, documentation, and developer ergonomics as first-class concerns, not afterthoughts.

Reliability is a feature. When platform engineers write code, thousands of engineers are affected by their choices. We hold ourselves to a higher standard of testing, rollout discipline, and incident response than application teams require of themselves.
