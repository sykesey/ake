Project Status Report: Churn Prediction Model

Project ID: PR003 | Team: Data Science (T003) | Status: Active | As of: 2025-04-14

Timeline: 2025-01-01 to 2025-07-31 | Budget: $68,000

Executive Summary

The Churn Prediction Model project is ahead of plan on ML development and entering the serving infrastructure phase. Champion model selected (AUC 0.884 on holdout, exceeding the 0.85 target). The team is on track for a May production deployment, two weeks ahead of the original schedule.

Model Performance Summary

  • Algorithm: XGBoost (max_depth=6, learning_rate=0.05, n_estimators=400)
  • Holdout AUC: 0.884 (target: ≥0.85)
  • Precision at top-10% risk decile: 71%
  • Recall at top-10% risk decile: 68%
  • Training dataset: 18 months of customer activity, 147,000 labelled examples
  • Feature count: 87 features including 12 derived real-time behavioural signals
  • Training time: 4.2 hours on 8-core GPU instance

The pilot will target the 200 accounts with the highest predicted churn probability. Customer Success Lead Skyler Brown (E012) will co-own the pilot with Taylor Nguyen. Success criteria: Customer Success team reports that model scores are actionable and directionally correct for ≥80% of flagged accounts.
