PR005: Real-time Analytics Pipeline — Architecture Decision

The team reviewed three stream processing options and made a final decision:

  • Kafka Streams: rejected — JVM operational complexity, limited exactly-once guarantees
  • Apache Flink (managed): selected — exactly-once semantics, managed offering removes operational burden
  • Custom asyncio consumer: rejected — insufficient throughput guarantees for billing events

Jamie will own the Flink prototype. Target: end-to-end proof of concept by 2025-05-01. Taylor to document the ADR for this decision and circulate for comment by 2025-04-18.
