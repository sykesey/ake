Data Privacy and Compliance

Training data is drawn from pseudonymised customer activity logs. No PII (name, email, address) is used as a model feature. The model outputs a risk score per account_id; mapping to customer name occurs only at the point of Customer Success review and is subject to existing data access controls. Legal sign-off required before pilot launch.
